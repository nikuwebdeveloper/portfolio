about

My work is split in two: professional web development and independent game development. The idea behind both is a strong belief that technology should respect its user. Too many modern websites and games are frustrating to use, whether due to oversight or design flaws. For my web projects, I create straightforward websites. In game development, I use retro aesthetics and solid mechanics. I use foundations of games of the past as a place to start experimenting. You will find professional and proof of concept web projects here as well as my games. I am looking for a full-time position or freelance clients in web development.
